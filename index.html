Index construction and measurement notes 指数构建与方法说明

China Financial Institutions Digital Capability Index 中国金融机构数字化能力指数

The index is constructed from public annual reports, corporate social responsibility reports, and other disclosure texts. It operationalizes a financial digital capability maturity framework into eight domains and 75 item-level scores, which are aggregated into an institution-year panel. 指数基于金融机构年报、社会责任报告和其他公开披露文本构建，将金融数字化能力成熟度框架转化为八个能力域、75 个能力项的分项评分，并汇总形成机构-年份层面的面板数据。

Measurement procedure测度流程

From public disclosures to an institution-year index 公开披露文本的指数化流程

The construction procedure separates sample registration, file mapping, domain scoring, structured parsing, score aggregation, and validation checks. The purpose is to show how lengthy public reports are converted into comparable numerical indicators. 测度流程按样本登记、文件映射、领域评分、结构化解析、指数合并和有效性检验展开，说明长篇公开报告如何转换为可比较的数值指标。

Sample frame样本框架

The sample covers banks, insurers, and securities firms with available public reports during 2010-2025. Institution names are standardized before report-level scores are consolidated.样本覆盖 2010-2025 年具有公开报告的银行、保险和证券机构。报告级评分合并前，先对机构名称进行标准化处理。

Document handling文本处理

Each report keeps its original file path, report type, institution-year identifiers, and remote file identifier. This file ledger is used to connect score records back to source documents.每份报告保留原始路径、报告类型、机构-年份标识和远端文件 ID，用于把评分记录与来源文件对应起来。

Scoring protocol评分协议

The scoring instructions require item-level integers from 0 to 5, item-specific rationales, and a strict JSON structure. Ambiguous or generic statements are not treated as high-level capability evidence.评分指令要求输出 0 到 5 的分项整数分、逐项评分理由和严格 JSON 结构。模糊表述或泛化口号不作为高档位能力依据。

Aggregation rule合并规则

When multiple reports are available for the same institution-year, item-level scores are consolidated before domain and total indices are computed. The public release reports the total index and eight domain scores.同一机构-年份存在多份报告时，先在能力项层面合并，再计算能力域指数和总指数。公开版发布总指数和八个能力域得分。

01

Register reports样本登记

Create a stable manifest with institution name, year, report type, original path, and a unique result identifier.按机构、年份、报告类型和原始路径生成清单，每份文件保留唯一 `result_id`。

02

Map files文件映射

Upload report PDFs and retain the mapping between local file paths, remote file identifiers, and institution metadata.将 PDF 上传为远端 `file_id`，并保留 `file_id`、本地路径与机构元数据的映射关系。

03

Score by domain领域评分

Run eight domain-specific scoring tasks, each focused on one capability domain and its item-level criteria.八个领域测度任务分别读取全文，在对应能力域内完成原文识别、档位判断和逐项评分。

04

Constrain source basis原文依据约束

Require source-grounded rationales. If no specific disclosure supports an item, the item is assigned zero.每次调用要求输出分项 0-5 整数分和评分理由；缺少明确原文依据时按 0 分处理。

05

Parse structured output结构化解析

Parse strict JSON and keep request identifiers, token usage, elapsed time, missing fields, and raw responses.脚本解析严格 JSON，并记录 `chat_id`、token、耗时、缺失项和原始响应。

06

Merge reports报告合并

Combine multiple reports for the same institution-year at the item level before computing domain indices.同一机构-年份存在多份公开报告时，按能力项保留更高披露依据对应的分数。

07

Standardize indices标准化

Normalize domain scores by the maximum possible item score, then compute the total index over all 75 items.能力域指数按本域最高可能分标准化，总指数对 75 个能力项采用同一计算逻辑。

08

Check validity质量控制

Check score ranges, rationale fields, file mappings, spot-check samples, external criteria, and keyword benchmarks.执行分数范围、理由文本、路径映射、人工抽样、外部效标和关键词法对照检查。

Domain-level measurement分项测度

Measurement design across eight capability domains 八个能力域的测度分工

The eight domains follow the regulatory maturity framework. Each domain has separate item definitions, tiered standards, and source-basis requirements, which reduces category mixing across governance, infrastructure, data, service delivery, and risk management. 八个能力域对应监管成熟度框架。每个领域分别设置能力项定义、分级标准和原文依据要求，以减少治理、基础设施、数据、服务和风险管理等类别的混淆。

Domain 1 · 9 items领域一 · 9 项

Fintech Governance System金融科技治理体系

  • Focus: organizational coordination, strategic planning, digital culture, ecosystem building, technology ethics, and responsibility mechanisms.关注组织协调、战略规划、数字文化、生态建设、科技伦理和主体责任。
  • High scores require explicit committees, formal plans, implementation mechanisms, or named governance arrangements.高档位通常要求披露委员会、正式规划、执行机制或明确治理安排。
Domain 2 · 8 items领域二 · 8 项

Data Factor Potential数据要素潜能释放

  • Focus: data strategy, governance, architecture, standards, quality, applications, sharing, and security.关注数据规划、治理体系、数据架构、数据标准、数据质量、数据应用、共享和安全。
  • The task separates broad claims about data from concrete systems, standards, quality controls, and business applications.测度时区分泛化数据表述与具体系统、标准、质量控制和业务应用。
Domain 3 · 13 items领域三 · 13 项

New Digital Infrastructure新型数字基础设施

  • Focus: data centers, disaster recovery, security operations, green efficiency, networks, IPv6, cloud, and secure computing.关注数据中心、容灾、安全、运维、绿色能效、网络、IPv6、云和安全计算。
  • Higher tiers require deployment scale, resilience, automation, or coverage metrics rather than isolated pilot language.高档位通常要求部署规模、韧性、自动化或覆盖率信息，而不是孤立试点表述。
Domain 4 · 7 items领域四 · 7 项

Core Technology Application关键核心技术应用

  • Focus: industry-research collaboration, supply-chain stability, proprietary control, distributed architecture, cloud migration, and value creation.关注产学研合作、供应链稳定、核心技术掌控、分布式架构、云化和价值创造。
  • The scoring distinguishes technology tracking from actual platform deployment or externally observable service output.评分区分技术跟踪、平台落地和可观察的服务输出。
Domain 5 · 12 items领域五 · 12 项

Digital Operating Momentum数字化经营动能

  • Focus: agile development, middle-platform construction, automated risk prevention, and intelligent marketing.关注敏捷创新、中台建设、自动化风险防控和数智化营销能力。
  • The domain captures operational embedding of digital systems rather than general technology investment.该领域强调数字系统嵌入经营流程，而不是一般性科技投入。
Domain 6 · 11 items领域六 · 11 项

Financial Service Reengineering金融服务智慧再造

  • Focus: process reengineering, omnichannel services, small business finance, rural finance, supply-chain finance, green finance, and inclusive access.关注流程再造、线上线下渠道、小微、农村、供应链、绿色和无障碍服务。
  • High scores require evidence that digital tools change service delivery, not only that online channels exist.高档位要求数字工具改变服务交付方式，而不只是存在线上渠道。
Domain 7 · 11 items领域七 · 11 项

Prudent Fintech Management金融科技审慎管理

  • Focus: supervisory technology, compliance data, risk control, risk information repositories, new technology governance, and algorithm management.关注监管科技、合规数据、风控能力、风险信息库、新技术和算法管理。
  • The scoring favors documented monitoring, controls, risk databases, and lifecycle governance over generic compliance statements.评分更重视监测、控制、风险信息库和全生命周期治理，而非泛化合规表述。
Domain 8 · 4 items领域八 · 4 项

Sustainable Development Foundation可持续化发展基础

  • Focus: fintech talent selection, talent training, standard setting, and standard implementation.关注金融科技人才选育、标准制定和标准实施。
  • The domain measures capability building that supports long-run digital transformation.该领域衡量支撑长期数字化转型的能力建设基础。

system: Score financial digital capability only from the source report and the given tier definitions.

user: <domain-specific scoring rubric> + <strict JSON output requirement>

Scoring rules评分规则

Constraints imposed on every item评分约束

  • Each item must receive an integer score from 0 to 5.每个指标必须给出 0 到 5 的整数分。
  • Higher tiers normally require that lower-tier conditions are also met.较高档位默认要求较低档位全部满足。
  • The scoring basis must come from the source report, preferably with page, section, paragraph, or table location.评分依据必须来自报告原文，尽量定位到页码、章节、段落或表格。
  • Weakly related digital wording or generic slogans are not used for high-tier scores.与数字化关系弱或无明确披露的内容不纳入高档位评分。
  • Institution size, industry reputation, or common knowledge cannot substitute for source disclosures.不能根据机构规模、行业常识或泛化口号推断高档位。

Role and task boundary角色与任务边界

The instruction asks the model to act as a financial digital capability evaluator and limits scoring to the supplied report and rubric.指令要求模型以金融数字化能力测度者的身份工作，并限定评分只能依据报告文本和给定标准。

Item definitions and tier descriptions指标定义与档位说明

Each prompt contains the capability item definitions and tiered requirements that translate qualitative disclosure into a 0-5 maturity score.每份提示词包含能力项定义和分级要求，把定性披露转化为 0-5 的成熟度评分。

Negative and non-upgrade criteria未升档条件

The instructions require a low score when evidence is absent, vague, only planned, or unrelated to the target capability item.当披露缺失、表述模糊、仅为规划或与能力项关系较弱时，指令要求保持低档位。

Illustrative scoring records评分案例

How item-level evidence is translated into scores能力项评分如何形成

The following examples summarize common scoring patterns observed in the measurement workflow. They are shown as condensed methodological examples rather than full quotations from individual reports. 以下案例概括测度过程中常见的评分模式，作为方法展示中的压缩示例，而不是逐字引用某一份报告。

Case A · Data standards案例 A · 数据标准

From general data governance to measurable data-standard capability从数据治理表述到数据标准能力评分

Relevant disclosure相关披露
Mentions enterprise data governance, data dictionaries, unified data standards, and platform-based quality checks.披露企业级数据治理、数据字典、统一数据标准和平台化质量检查。
Scoring judgment评分判断
A mid-to-high tier is considered only when the report moves beyond policy language and describes implementation, coverage, or evaluation mechanisms.只有当报告超出制度表述，并披露落地情况、覆盖范围或评价机制时，才考虑中高档位。
Non-upgrade condition未升档条件
No higher tier is assigned if the report does not describe quantitative monitoring, dynamic assessment, or business-value measurement of standards.若未披露量化监测、动态考核或数据标准业务价值度量，则不进入更高档位。
Case B · Digital infrastructure案例 B · 数字基础设施

Distinguishing infrastructure deployment from exploratory pilots区分基础设施落地与探索性试点

Relevant disclosure相关披露
Describes cloud platforms, disaster recovery, security operations, IPv6 deployment, or automated operations and maintenance.披露云平台、容灾、安全运营、IPv6 部署或自动化运维。
Scoring judgment评分判断
Deployment scope, system coverage, resilience, and continuity arrangements are used to separate mature infrastructure from early pilots.根据部署范围、系统覆盖、韧性和连续性安排，区分成熟基础设施与早期试点。
Non-upgrade condition未升档条件
Statements such as "exploring cloud migration" or "planning security upgrades" are not sufficient for high-tier scores without implementation details.“探索云迁移”“规划安全升级”等表述若缺少落地细节，不足以支持高档位评分。
Case C · Service reengineering案例 C · 服务智慧再造

Evaluating whether digital tools change service delivery判断数字工具是否改变服务交付

Relevant disclosure相关披露
Mentions mobile services, intelligent customer service, supply-chain finance platforms, green finance data labels, or inclusive accessibility functions.披露移动服务、智能客服、供应链金融平台、绿色金融数据标签或无障碍功能。
Scoring judgment评分判断
Higher scores require process redesign, channel integration, or service personalization rather than a simple online-channel description.高档位要求流程再造、渠道融合或服务个性化，而不只是线上渠道描述。
Non-upgrade condition未升档条件
A basic mobile app or online banking channel alone is treated as a low-level capability unless service logic is materially changed.仅披露手机银行或网银渠道，若没有服务逻辑变化，一般只对应低档位能力。
Case D · Prudent fintech management案例 D · 审慎管理

Separating risk-management infrastructure from generic compliance language区分风控基础设施与一般合规表述

Relevant disclosure相关披露
Describes risk data marts, model-risk processes, algorithm governance, cybersecurity monitoring, or compliance data infrastructure.披露风险数据集市、模型风险流程、算法治理、网络安全监测或合规数据基础设施。
Scoring judgment评分判断
A higher tier is considered when governance is embedded in monitoring, approval, reporting, and lifecycle management.当治理安排嵌入监测、审批、报告和全生命周期管理时，才考虑更高档位。
Non-upgrade condition未升档条件
Broad references to "strengthening risk control" are not enough without a system, database, or operating process.仅表述“加强风险控制”而缺少系统、数据库或运行流程，不足以支持高档位评分。

Index formula指数计算

Domain and total index calculation能力域和总指数计算

The domain index equals the sum of item scores in the domain divided by the maximum possible score in that domain. The total index applies the same normalization logic to all 75 capability items.能力域指数等于本域实际分数之和除以该域最高可能分数。总指数对 75 个能力项采用同一标准化逻辑。

Domain = Σ item score / (5 × item count)
Overall = Σ 75 item scores / (5 × 75)

The public release reports institution-year total indices and eight domain scores. Internal process tables retain scoring rationales, file paths, result identifiers, and file identifiers.公开版只发布机构-年份指数和八个能力域得分；内部过程表保留评分原因、文件路径、`result_id` 和 `file_id`。

Quality checks and validity analysis质量控制与有效性检验

Quality control and external validation质量控制与外部验证

The project uses structured checks, manual spot checks, fintech patent outcomes, keyword-based benchmarks, and item-level remeasurement to identify potential bias from disclosure intensity and automated scoring errors. 为降低披露强度差异和自动评分误差的影响，本项目结合结构化校验、人工抽样、金融科技专利、关键词法对照和单项重测识别潜在偏差。

Structured checks结构化校验

The main score table and file index each contain 8,804 rows. Rationale cells are non-empty in 70,064 of 70,432 report-domain cells, a coverage rate of 99.48%.主表和文件索引均为 8,804 行；原因单元格 70,064 / 70,432 非空，覆盖率 99.48%。

Manual spot checks人工抽样校验

The spot-check design covers high, medium, and low scores; banks, securities firms, and insurers; and both annual reports and CSR reports.覆盖高分、中分、低分，覆盖银行、证券、保险，以及年报和社会责任报告。

Risk-expression review可疑表述核查

Records containing expressions similar to "inferred", "not directly disclosed", or "close to the requirement" are flagged, especially when the associated score is high.对“推断、未直接、接近”等高风险表述做二次标记，优先检查高分且含推断性理由的样本。

Item-level remeasurement单项指标重测

For the data-standard item, 7,214 records were remeasured and fully parsed, with retry and manual override records retained.针对 2-4 数据标准做全量重测，7,214 条记录最终全部解析完成，并保留两轮补跑和人工覆盖记录。

External criterion外部效标检验

The fintech patent validation sample matches 41 banks and 384 bank-year observations. Annual patent flow and the index mean have a correlation of 0.855.金融科技专利样本匹配 41 家银行、384 个银行-年份观测；年度专利流量与指数均值相关系数为 0.855。

Keyword benchmark关键词法基准

A traditional dictionary-based measure is retained as an independent benchmark, including dictionary construction, OCR status, low-text-quality flags, and frequency-index generation.传统关键词法作为独立基准，记录词典、OCR、低文本质量标记和频率指数生成过程。

Scope of interpretation适用边界

The index measures digital capability information disclosed in public texts. It does not equate slogan-like statements with actual capability, and it does not interpret correlations as strong causal evidence. External criteria and alternative methods supplement, rather than replace, report-level scoring bases. 指数测量的是公开披露文本中的数字化能力信息，不把口号式表述等同于真实能力，也不把相关性结果表述为强因果结论。外部效标和替代方法用于增强有效性，而不是替代报告级评分依据。
